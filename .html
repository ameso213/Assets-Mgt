Depreciation

Configure depreciation methods and rates. Applied per asset category to compute current book value.
NameMethodRate / YearsApplies ToAssets UsingActions
How Depreciation Works
Assets are assigned a depreciation method at registration. The system uses the purchase date and cost to compute the current book value. Straight-Line reduces value evenly each year. Declining Balance applies a fixed percentage to the remaining value. Sum-of-Years front-loads depreciation.
